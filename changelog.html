What's new

Product updates during early access.

July 2026

  • Connection status & firmware. Device pages now show a live "refreshed" indicator, your device's firmware version, and a banner if the connection drops.
  • Friendlier account flows. Clearer confirmations after registering or resetting your password, plus a confirm-password field when creating an account.
  • Dashboard at a glance. Each device card shows when it was last seen, with a tidier layout and a clearer empty state.
  • Mobile navigation. A collapsing menu makes the app easier to use on phones.
  • Faster, safer controls. Styled confirmation dialogs for actions like Force Off and deleting a device.

June 2026

  • Light & dark mode. The site follows your system theme and remembers your choice.
  • Schedule commands. Queue a power-on, power-off, or reset for a future time.
  • Temperature history. A 7-day temperature chart on each device page.
  • One-click firmware flashing. Flash your ESP32 straight from the setup page in Chrome or Edge — no software to install.
  • Account management. Change your email or password, and delete your account, from the account page.
