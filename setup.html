Setup Guide

Early access: Full firmware documentation and a flashing tool are coming soon. These steps outline the setup flow — some parts are still being finalised.
1

Create an account

Register at app.onoffmypc.com/register with your email address and a password.

2

Register your device

In the app, add a new device and give it a name. You'll receive a unique device token — keep this safe, it's how your ESP32 authenticates to the service.

3

Flash the ESP32

Flash the OnOffMyPC firmware onto your ESP32. During setup, enter your Wi-Fi credentials and your device token. The device will connect to the service automatically on boot.

4

Wire to your PC

Connect the ESP32 output pin to your PC's power switch header on the motherboard. Enable Wake-on-LAN in your BIOS/UEFI settings and your network adapter power management options.

5

You're done

Open the app, check that your device shows as online, and try sending a power command. Telemetry (power state, temperature, humidity) will appear in the dashboard within seconds.
